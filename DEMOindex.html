About Us

My name is Aditya and I'm the founder of The Waste Address, a consulting business based in Bengaluru, KA. We are focused on providing businesses with help in understanding and managing their waste issues. Our team of consultants is experienced in helping companies reduce their waste and increase their sustainability.We provide our clients with a range of services, from waste audits to waste management plans. We also offer waste analysis services to help identify areas of improvement in their waste management practices. Our team of experts can advise on the best waste management solutions for each situation, taking into consideration factors like the local regulations, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact. We strive to help our clients become more efficient and sustainable in their waste management practices.
